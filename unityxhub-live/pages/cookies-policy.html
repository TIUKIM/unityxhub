This Cookies Policy explains how UnityXhub.com.ph (“UnityXhub,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website and use our services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website.

They help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve your experience
  • Provide secure and personalized services

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  1. Essential Cookies
    • Required for the website to function properly.
    • Example: Login sessions, security features, payment processing.
  2. Performance Cookies
    • Collect information about how users interact with the website.
    • Example: Traffic analytics, page speed.
  3. Functionality Cookies
    • Remember user preferences.
    • Example: Language settings, saved searches, shopping carts.
  4. Advertising & Marketing Cookies
    • Used to show relevant ads and measure their effectiveness.
    • Example: Google Ads, Facebook Pixel.

3. Why We Use Cookies

  • ✅ Improve security & fraud prevention
  • ✅ Save user login & preferences
  • ✅ Analyze performance & traffic
  • ✅ Provide personalized ads and recommendations

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may come from third-party partners such as:

  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Payment processors (PayPal, Stripe, local e-wallets, banks)
  • Advertising platforms (Facebook Ads, Google Ads, TikTok Ads)

We do not control these third-party cookies. Please review their individual privacy policies.

5. Cookie Management & Opt-Out

You can:

  • Manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • Decline non-essential cookies via our cookie banner/pop-up.
  • Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality (e.g., login, cart).

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a new “Effective Date.”
